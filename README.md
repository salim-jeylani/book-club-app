# 📚 Book Club App

A web application for book lovers to track what they're reading, discover new books, and share reviews with others.

## What It Does

**Book Club App** gives readers a personal space to manage their reading life and connect with others around books they love.

- **Track your reading** — Mark books as *Want to Read*, *Currently Reading*, or *Finished*, and log your progress page by page
- **Discover books** — Browse a shared library, search by title or author, and filter by genre
- **Share your thoughts** — Write reviews, leave star ratings, and read what others think before picking up your next book
- **Your personal shelf** — A dedicated page showing all your books organised by reading status at a glance

## Why I Built It

I wanted a focused, real-world project that covered the full lifecycle of a web application — from user accounts and a database, through to a polished interface and deployment. Books felt like a natural fit: the domain is familiar, but the features (user authentication, relational data, search, reviews) are genuinely interesting to build well.

## What I Learned

Building this end to end meant making real decisions at every layer — how to structure the database, how to handle user sessions securely, how to keep the codebase organised as it grows. It pushed me to think not just about making things *work*, but making them *right*.

## Project Status

Currently in active development. Core features are complete; testing and deployment are in progress.
